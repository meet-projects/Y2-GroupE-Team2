     Ukko’s computational engineering platform
    re-designs food allergens, turning them into safe and efficacious therapeutics that will become best in class therapy for allergy     

    We are building a comprehensive, clinically validated map of the molecular structure of food allergies, unlocking critical knowledge about the biological mechanisms that drive human-food interactions.

    On the basis of this map, we use  platform to design new versions of the proteins that do not trigger an immune response. These proteins allow a whole new approach for how we treat protein based allergies.
